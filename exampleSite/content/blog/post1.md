Site configuration
You will see params.toml config.toml and menu.toml file in the exampleSite folder.

params.toml
params.toml which gives you the ability to configure your site, such as changing logo, Favicon.

Changing logo and logo-height
logo = "images/logo.png"
logo_width = "100x"
Changing Favicon
favicon = "images/favicon.png"
